What is our Organization About?

SK Children Foundation is a non profit organization(NGO). Our organization works 365 days in a year We teach and provide best education to those who cannot afford it. Our aim is to provide education to those who will be the future of our COUNTRY. We do not discriminate on the basis of cast, gender, etc. OUR MISSION is to bring smile on every little face by educating them ,teaching him/her good values. Everyone deserves education. By teaching these poor children we want to end poverty. We want to see every child of Our Country to achieve good education and for this we are working on it. In our country education is not available free of cost. Poor people cannot afford to pay high tuition fees in private schools. The sad part is the lack of quality in government schools. There is a lack of proper infrastructure, punctuality and motivation among the teachers. So the number of dropouts from such schools keeps increasing and the children end up working as labourers and remain in poverty for the rest of their lives. We want to drag them out from the darkness to the path of light full of education, better future and most importantly good and a happy life.

How It All Started

In 2016, Raghav Sharma (Founder Of SKCF), a class 12 th student, was buying Ice Cream. He saw group of children who came near him and requested him to buy ice-cream for them. Raghav asked them if they will recite English Alphabets, then he will buy them a treat.When they failed in reciting the alphabets , he realised that still living in 21st Century, Education is not available to all. He believed that he must be the change he wants to see in the society. Hence, he took immediate action and started teaching a couple of kids around the neighbourhood in a nearby park in Janakpuri. He hasn’t looked back ever since. The number of kids have grown from one to 150+, new dedicated members have joined the cause, volunteers have helped on many occasions and numerous events have already been held. In 2019, the NGO was formally registered as SK Children Foundation as a public charitable trust. Contact us on the mentioned email or instagram and facebook pages to connect with us. All the pictures and logs of our activities can be checked on our Instagram and Facebook pages: skchildrenfoundation.

What We Do?

1. We TEACH

Our classes are conducted all 7 days a week in the morning or evening in Janakpuri park, New Delhi.
We teach subjects like Maths, English, Hindi, Social sciences and more to our children who are in the 6-14 age group and come from poor backgrounds.

2. We STRENGTHEN

Due to the age differences, we conduct adaptive classes where each faculty member teaches a few students based on his/her level.
Our hard working members make sure each and every child understands the concept well.

3. We PLAY

On Sundays, we conduct an activity day where all students play sports like kho-kho, football, cricket, bowling, etc. which help them enjoy and learn.
We also give coloring books to draw and colour, and conduct special meditation sessions for all the kids.

4. We PROVIDE FOOD

We serve healthy meals and refreshments to the students on a regular basis. Many a time we also provide lunch during classes.
Special sweets, gifts, stationary, bags are distributed during festivals and events. All such expenses are funded by us as of now.

5. ENJOY FESTIVALS

We conduct special events for every festival, be it Diwali, Holi, Dussehra, Gurupurab, Gandhi Jayanti and many more.
We focus on teaching the significance and values associated with each festival and how to implement them in our life.

6. We HAVE FUN

We want that students should learn while having fun. Hence we conduct educational picnics for students’ learning and enjoyment.
Recently we have conducted excursions to Aeroplanet Dwarka, Mughal Gardens, India Gate among others.
